# Kitaab App Account Deletion Request

To request deletion of your Kitaab account and associated data (including audio recordings):

1. Email us at your-support-email@example.com from the email address linked to your account.
2. Include your full name and account email in the subject line: "Kitaab Account Deletion Request".
3. We will verify your identity and permanently delete your account, profile, and all recordings from our servers within 7 days.
4. You will receive a confirmation email once complete.

**Note:** This process complies with Google Play requirements. For privacy questions, see our [Privacy Policy](your-privacy-policy-url-here).
